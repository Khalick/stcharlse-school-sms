Simple, Intelligent, and Completely Connected

We have designed a streamlined school management platform focused strictly on five core modules: automating teacher timetables, direct parent notifications, structured lesson vaults, twice-daily attendance synchronization (Morning Check-In and Evening Check-Out), and a private AI study companion for students. No complex accounting panels, just tools that keep St. Charles School synchronized.

📂 2. Student & Teacher Directory

A unified digital record-keeper holding all critical profile details in one secure place.

  • ✓
    Searchable Profiles

    Type a student or teacher's name to instantly reveal their assigned class, contact details, emergency guardians, and historical attendance rates.

  • ✓
    Admissions Helper

    Register new incoming students in less than a minute. Specify their assigned stream, parent telephone numbers, and notes.

💡 Student Directory Verification (Case Scenario)

Step 1: Parent Inquiry
A guardian calls the school office to check if their child has settled in on the first day of the term.
Step 2: Instant Search
The secretary types the student's name (e.g., "David Kamau") into the search box.
Step 3: Verification
The system immediately displays David's profile card, showing he is marked present in Class 7A, sits in Room 4, and lists his father's active Safaricom number. The entire verification takes only 5 seconds.

📥 3. Teacher Workspace & Twice-Daily Attendance

A focused hub designed to help teachers manage their schedules, upload course materials, and log student attendance twice daily.

  • ✓
    Morning Check-In Registry

    First thing in the morning (by 8:30 AM), teachers mark their morning registers. This gives the school an instant account of who has arrived safely.

  • ✓
    Evening Check-Out Registry

    Before leaving the school in the evening (by 4:00 PM), teachers mark their evening registers. This accounts for student safety before they board buses or walk home.

  • ✓
    Resource & Notes Publisher

    Teachers can upload study notes, classroom slides, past exams, or homework sheets. They simply drag the file, choose the subject (e.g. English), and click publish.

🏫 The Morning & Evening Attendance Cycle (Case Scenario)

Step 1: Morning Check-In (8:10 AM)
Teacher Agnes enters the Grade 7 Science classroom. She opens her portal, clicks "Morning Check-In", marks David present and Joseph absent, and submits. The Admin board immediately registers David's safe arrival.
Step 2: Classroom Monitoring
During the day, David is in class. If any emergency occurs, the office directory confirms David's presence.
Step 3: Evening Check-Out (3:55 PM)
Before classes end, Agnes opens her portal again. The dashboard locks other panels until the "Evening Check-Out" register is submitted. She marks all present students as they board the buses or head home. Once submitted, the Admin panel turns Agnes's icon green, showing 100% attendance tracking compliance.

  • ✓

    Students access their study files sorted by subject. No stress-inducing grade books or payment reminders are visible in this workspace.

  • ✓
    "Charlie" AI Assistant

    A friendly chat bubble next to the study material. It reads the note content and answers students' questions, explaining complex topics in simple terms.

  • ✓
    Talk & Listen Controls

💬 5. Automated Reminders & Three-Channel Parental Messaging

Aligns school timetables with automated notifications, ensuring teachers are in class and parents are kept updated via WhatsApp, Safaricom SMS, and Email.

Direct-to-Parent Triple Pathway:

💬
WhatsApp Delivery

Sends an instant, rich template message straight to the parent's WhatsApp app. It features the school crest, formatted text, and visual ticks showing delivery.

📱
Safaricom SMS

Ensures immediate alerts reach parents' phones even when mobile data is switched off, using Safaricom's local carrier network.

✉️
Email Delivery

Sends a complete, styled copy of the announcement to the parent's inbox, ideal for long-term records and sharing detailed handouts.

  • ✓
    Automated Schedule Reminders

    Upload the school's timetable file. The system parses all hours and classrooms. Exactly 5 minutes before any class, a reminder pops up on the assigned teacher's device.

  • ✓
    Multi-Channel Broadcaster

    Type one announcement, and the system sends it via WhatsApp, SMS, and Email simultaneously with a single click.

⏰ The 5-Minute Class Warning (Case Scenario)

Step 1: Boundary Watch
At 9:55 AM, the system scheduler checks the timetable and notices Grade 7 has a Kiswahili lesson starting at 10:00 AM with Teacher Mark in Room 12.
Step 2: Alert Dispatch
Teacher Mark's smartphone vibrates, showing a push reminder: "Kiswahili Class starting in 5 minutes in Room 12."
Step 3: Verification
Mark receives the warning and walks to Room 12, starting his class exactly on time.

📢 Emergency Parent Alert (Case Scenario)

Step 1: Admin Entry
Due to weather conditions, the school director decides to release students early. The admin types a broadcast: "Dear parents, school will close early today at 1:00 PM due to local rainfall. Transport buses have departed."
Step 2: Triple-Channel Dispatch
The admin clicks "Send Broadcast". Instantly:
  • WhatsApp: Parent James gets a WhatsApp message with double ticks.
  • Safaricom SMS: Parent James's phone rings with an SMS alert (useful if his data is turned off).
  • Email: An official email copy is sent to his inbox for confirmation.
